I’m a senior researcher with a Ph.D. in Human-Computer Interaction. Currently, I work at BMW developing in-car user interfaces and interaction concepts. Since 2009, I’ve contributed to numerous projects for both digital and analog mediums, earning recognition from the German Design Award Council. I collaborate with ‘Atelier Brieftaube’ on a project-based basis and occasionally give lectures.

If you are curious, I also curate a database where I collect resources that inspire my research and practice. But enough about me, now tell me about your project!
